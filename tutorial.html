Welcome to Typing Hero!

In this game, you type the sentence that appears on-screen. There are three difficulty levels, ranging from relatively normal-looking sentences to random characters, and each difficulty grants a different number of experience points (xp). For each character incorrectly typed (this does include spaces), your xp reward will decrease slightly, but at least 50% of the possible total reward will be given once you finish the sentence.

As you earn experience, you will also gain levels (leveling up). Leveling up happens frequently at lower levels, but gets more difficult as your level gets higher.

Hope you have fun playing Typing Hero!
